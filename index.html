Game rule
X

1. Users use the Road Paver to draw the road that citizens can walk on.

2. When a citizen enters a wrong destination, if the player places a rebirth gate, the citizen will reborn at the rebirth gate and walk along the road connected(Only once). When the citizen go back to the rebirth gate for some reason, they can not enter the rebirth gate and turn around and continue to walk along the road.

3. The rebirth gate can only be placed on the road and must be at one end of the road. It can connect roads in any direction, but it can only connect roads in one direction at the same time.

4. Navigator can only be placed on intersection/junction. When citizen enter the navigator, they will continue to walk towards the branch which can lead to the destination. If there is no road leading to the desired destination, they will randomly choose a direction(Except for turn around) and continue to walk;

5. When players use Remove tool, the tool placed above the road will be removed first, and then the road will be removed when clicking again.
